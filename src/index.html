What is a Home Inspection?

A window into the workings of your home

A Home inspection is a non-invasive, visual examination of a residential dwelling and it provides you with a written report that identifies any defect that changes the value of the home.

It's not always easy for an anxious home buyer to look past the cosmetic aspects of a home. As a trained and experienced home inspector we can provide an unbiased, non-emotional evaluation of of the homeby checking hundreds of components for Safety, Operation, and Condition.

A clear and concise report follows every home inspection. These are easy to read and can be used as a reference manual in the future.

Home Inspections are Important

Because buying a home can be stressful

Buying a home can be stressful, but we can help. By getting a home inspection you can remove a lot of the worry and confusion associated with the home buying process. We provide you with a professional look into the workings of your potential home. Buying a home may be the biggest decision you'll make, so we provide you with the proper knowledge to help you make an informed decision.

Home inspections are a smart choice!

Additional Services

What else do I need?

Home inspections come in all shapes and sizes. During the course of a home inspection we may recommend additional checks to important components in your home. These services are equally important as the basic home inspection and may save you of money if a problem is identified or may be required by your mortgage company.

The additional services we can provide are:

  • Radon Screening
  • Water Quality Screening
  • Water Flow Testings
  • Wood Destroying Insects (Termites, ants, etc...)
